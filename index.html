Executive Summary

Based on our comprehensive analysis of over 1 million job seeker posts across major platforms, we've documented 127 verified ghosting incidents across 71 companies. The data reveals that employer ghosting has evolved from an occasional discourtesy into a systemic breakdown in the hiring process.

The practice has more than doubled since 2020, with 61% of job seekers now reporting they have been ghosted after an interview. In 2025 alone, 48% of job seekers experienced ghosting, up from 38% the previous year.
